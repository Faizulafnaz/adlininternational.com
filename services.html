  • GENERAL CONTRACTING

    When a company acts as a general contractor the whole process of work on the project is carefully planned and executed according to the schedule with the ability of the customer to control the key stages. Choosing a general contracting service, the customer saves considerable time and reduces the construction budget, and also avoids the risks arising from cooperation with several contractors.

  • DESIGN & BUILD

    A project delivery system whereby ADLIN provides full design including MEP and interior fit out services in-house under one single Turnkey solutions contract. A design and build contract has the potential to reduce overall project risks and costs, and may also result in earlier completion of a project due to one point of contact.

  • FITOUT

    ADLIN offers interior fit-outs for commercial, residential, retail, F&B and hospitality projects. Our team works relentlessly to deliver creative and cost-effective solutions to fulfil the client’s needs. By blending artistic interpretation, high-quality materials, and cutting-edge technology, we create interior environments that connect, inspire and captivate people. We have a proven track record working on all types of fit-out projects throughout the region. ADLIN takes pride in delivering the best results even when we face extremely tight and complex deadlines.

  • MEP Services

    We offers MEP maintenance services around, backed by its team of experienced, trained and efficient engineers, supervisors and technicians. This is combined with management skills to achieve customer satisfaction. This includes timely delivery of quality and reliable services, periodic audits of the entire MEP system, repairs and replacement of required parts and finally calibration of the system as well.

  • MECHANICAL
