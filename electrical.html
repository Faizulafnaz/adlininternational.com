Our expert team ensures every installation is safe, efficient, and fully compliant with Qatar General Electricity & Water Corporation (Kahramaa) and Qatar Construction Specifications (QCS).

Powering Your Projects with Precision

From small-scale wiring to large-scale electrical design and implementation, we provide intelligent and reliable systems tailored to your building's needs. Whether new construction or renovation, we deliver electrical works that meet modern safety and performance standards.

Our Electrical System Services Include

Precision-engineered solutions for safety, efficiency, and compliance in Qatar.

Electrical Design & Load Calculation

We provide detailed load assessments, single-line diagrams, and electrical layout planning for optimal energy use and distribution.

Internal & External Wiring

Professional installation of all types of wiring, including lighting, socket points, power outlets, and equipment connections, using high-grade materials and safety protocols.

Lighting Systems

We install ambient, task, emergency, and landscape lighting systems, along with smart control systems for homes, offices, hotels, and retail spaces.

Emergency & Exit Lighting

We implement Civil Defense-approved emergency lighting systems, ensuring visibility and compliance during power outages and evacuations.

DB & Panel Installation

We install and commission MDBs, SMDBs, and FDBs with complete safety checks and labeling for smooth electrical distribution.

Testing, Commissioning & Fault Diagnosis

All systems are thoroughly tested, commissioned, and fine-tuned for load balance, efficiency, and long-term performance.

Earthing & Lightning Protection

Installation of grounding systems and lightning arrestors for asset and life safety, meeting the latest QCS standards.
