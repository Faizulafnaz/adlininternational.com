Our Mechanical Services Include

HVAC Systems

We design and install high-performance Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems including ducted units, VRF/VRV systems, chillers, and split systems for efficient climate control.

AHUs & FAHUs

Expert installation and balancing of Air Handling Units and Fresh Air Handling Units to improve air quality, maintain airflow, and meet ventilation standards.

Fan Coil Units (FCUs)

Zone-specific cooling with Fan Coil Units to ensure personalized comfort in residential and commercial spaces.

Ventilation & Exhaust Systems

Complete systems for fresh air intake and exhaust, including kitchen exhausts, toilet exhausts, car park ventilation, and smoke extraction.

Fire Fighting Systems

Installation of fire sprinkler systems, hose reels, fire pumps, and fire-rated ducting — all certified and approved by Qatar Civil Defense (QCD).

Testing, Commissioning & Maintenance

We ensure each system is precisely calibrated, tested, and maintained for optimal performance, safety, and compliance.
