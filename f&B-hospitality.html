Our F&B & Hospitality Fit-Out Services Include

Restaurant & Café Fit-Out

We design and build interiors that reflect your cuisine, theme, and brand. Our services include dining layouts, décor, counters, kitchen setups, lighting, acoustic treatments, and ventilation.

Hotel & Serviced Apartment Interiors

From luxury lobbies to guest rooms, we offer full hotel interior fit-outs including reception areas, lounges, meeting rooms, corridors, and back-of-house spaces.

Commercial Kitchen Fit-Out

We install high-performance commercial kitchens with optimized layouts, exhaust systems, grease traps, drainage, and fire-rated walls, all in compliance with Qatar Civil Defense and Municipality codes.

MEP & HVAC Works

We handle complete Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing installations, including air conditioning, lighting control, sound systems, and smart energy solutions.
